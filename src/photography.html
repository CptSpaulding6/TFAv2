Photography

The present moment

Capturing the essence of being

Gadsby Namur is a gourmet burger restaurant, and they wanted to do a photo shoot of the staff in keeping with the concept and atmosphere of the restaurant.

To present the restaurant's team and provide portraits for the "Employee of the Month" game, a photo shoot was organised in the 1920s English atmosphere of the restaurant.

"Photography is a passion for me, I love to walk around and capture wildlife and nature, so when I was contacted about organising a photo shoot for portraits in an atypical atmosphere, I immediately accepted. It's a pleasure to broaden one's horizons, the team is great, the portraits have a real charm thanks to the decoration and the outfits, the atmosphere".
